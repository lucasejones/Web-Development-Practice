Make a wish! It just might come true.

"I may be small, but you could have it all!"

Have you ever wished for a better life? Wished you had more time, more success? More adulation?

Teeny Genie is an opportunity for you to obtain what you seek. More of the Mores you want, and less of the Lesses you don't.

Features

Unlimited Number of Wishes

Unlike standard genies, Teeny Genie's got stamina.

Pay as You Go

Spend only what you like

Sliding Outcome Scale

The more you use it, the better your chances


There's just one catch - there's a chance you may get the bad version of your wish. Think of it as the inverse of what you want. If you wish for breadsticks, if you get the bad version of your wish, you might get fishsticks. Or just some normal sticks.

How it Works

While Teeny Genie requires you to put up sufficient collateral for a given wish in the event of a bad outcome, the more you use Teeny Genie, the odds shift ever in your favor.

The randomization starts at 80% bad outcome chance, and 20% good. Every time you use Teeny Genie, your good chance improves 1% (1% of the total 100%, not of your current good percentage. This isn't a trick. Ultimtely, your odds for cap at 80% good outcome chance, and 20% bad outcome chance.
