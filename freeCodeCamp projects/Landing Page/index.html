How it Works

There's just one catch - there's a chance you may get the bad version of your wish. For example, if you wish for breadsticks, in the bad version you might get fishsticks. Or just some normal sticks.

While Teeny Genie requires you to put up sufficient collateral for a given wish in the event of a bad outcome, the more you use Teeny Genie, the odds shift ever in your favor.

The randomization starts at 80% bad outcome chance, and 20% good. Every time you use Teeny Genie, your good chance improves by 1% (1% of the total 100%, not of your current good percentage). Ultimately, your odds cap at a good outcome chance of 80%, and 20% chance for a bad outcome.
