How it Works

There's just one catch - there's a chance you may get the bad version of your wish. For example, if you wish for breadsticks, in the bad version you might get fishsticks. Or just some normal sticks.

While Teeny Genie requires you to put up sufficient collateral for a given wish in the event of a bad outcome*, each time you use Teeny Genie, the odds shift a teeny bit in your favor.**

Pricing

Due to an agreement we've made with the WISH foundation (Worldwide Illuminators of Supernatural Happenings), we're able to sell these incredible little magic-makers for only $49.99 per lamp. With free shipping to the contiguous United States, the only question is: can you afford not to try it?

"I may be small, but you could have it all!" - Teeny Genie

* Collateral is to be determined by Teeny Genie (TG) and agreed upon by the user prior to the granting of a wish. All outcomes are irrevocable regardless of user regret. TG is not obligated to make user's life better. Use of TG implies understanding of the concept that actions have consequences.

** The first time you use TG, you have an 80% bad outcome chance, 20% good. Every time after that, your good chance improves by 1% (absolute, not relative), ultimately maxing out at an 80% good outcome chance, 20% bad.
